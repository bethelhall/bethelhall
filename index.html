About Me

Bethel R. Hall is a doctoral researcher in the Department of Computer Science at Stevens Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. William J. Eiers. His research focuses on neurosymbolic methodologies, specifically integrating large language models into formal verification pipelines. By bridging machine learning, program synthesis, and automated reasoning, she seeks to develop systems that are both robustly intelligent and formally verifiable.
