Biography

I’m a CS Ph.D. student at Stevens Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. William Eiers. My research centers on neurosymbolic methods— integrating large language models into formal verification workflows. I work at the intersection of machine learning, program synthesis, and automated reasoning to build systems that are both intelligent and verifiable.
